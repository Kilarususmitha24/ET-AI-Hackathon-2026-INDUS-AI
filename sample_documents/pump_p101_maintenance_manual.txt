Pump P-101 Maintenance Manual
Document ID: MNT-P101-2024
Category: Maintenance

EQUIPMENT: Centrifugal Pump P-101
Location: Process Area A, Unit 3
Manufacturer: FlowTech Industries
Model: FT-CP-500

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE:
1. Weekly: Check bearing temperature (max 75°C), verify lubrication oil level
2. Monthly: Inspect mechanical seal for leakage, check vibration levels (ISO 10816 limit: 4.5 mm/s)
3. Quarterly: Replace oil filter, inspect impeller for wear, calibrate pressure sensors
4. Annually: Complete bearing replacement, shaft alignment check, performance test

LUBRICATION REQUIREMENTS:
- Bearing housing: ISO VG 68 turbine oil
- Grease fittings: Lithium complex grease NLGI Grade 2
- Oil change interval: 2000 operating hours

SAFETY PROCEDURES (OSHA 1910.147 Lockout/Tagout):
Before any maintenance on Pump P-101:
1. Notify control room of planned maintenance
2. Isolate electrical supply at MCC Panel 3A
3. Close suction valve V-101A and discharge valve V-101B
4. Apply lockout devices and tags to all energy isolation points
5. Verify zero energy state with pressure gauge reading 0 psig
6. Use appropriate PPE: safety glasses, gloves, steel-toe boots

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE:
- OSHA 1910.147: Energy Control Program compliance required
- API 610: Centrifugal Pump Standard for Petroleum Industry
- ISO 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management

INCIDENT HISTORY:
2024-03-15: Excessive vibration detected during peak load. Root cause: bearing wear due to insufficient lubrication. Corrective action: bearings replaced, lubrication schedule updated.

TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE:
Symptom: High vibration
- Check bearing condition and lubrication
- Verify shaft alignment
- Inspect impeller for damage or imbalance

Symptom: Low flow rate
- Check for clogged suction strainer
- Verify impeller wear
- Check motor RPM
