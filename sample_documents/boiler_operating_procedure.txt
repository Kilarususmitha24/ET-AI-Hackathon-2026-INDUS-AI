Boiler B-301 Operating Procedure
Document ID: SOP-B301-2024
Category: Procedure

EQUIPMENT: Industrial Boiler B-301
Type: Water Tube Boiler, 500 HP
Design Pressure: 250 psig
Location: Power House

STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE:

STARTUP SEQUENCE:
1. Verify all safety interlocks functional
2. Check water level in sight glass (normal: 50-75%)
3. Open feedwater valve FW-301
4. Purge combustion chamber for 5 minutes (4 volume changes minimum)
5. Ignite pilot burner, verify flame detection
6. Gradually increase firing rate, monitor steam pressure
7. Target operating pressure: 200 psig

SHUTDOWN SEQUENCE:
1. Reduce firing rate gradually over 30 minutes
2. Maintain water level during cooldown
3. Close fuel supply valves
4. Lockout per OSHA 1910.147 before internal inspection

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE:
- Daily: Blowdown bottom blow valves, check flame scanner
- Weekly: Test low water cutoff, inspect burner assembly
- Monthly: Water treatment analysis, safety valve bench test
- Annually: Internal inspection per API 510, tube thickness measurement

- NOx emissions: 45 ppm (limit: 50 ppm) - COMPLIANT

SAFETY REQUIREMENTS:
- NFPA 85: Boiler and Combustion Systems Hazards Code
- Confined space entry permit required for furnace inspection
- Hot work permit for welding repairs

TROUBLESHOOTING:
Low steam pressure: Check fuel supply, burner condition, feedwater temperature
High stack temperature: Clean fire-side surfaces, check excess air ratio
Water level fluctuations: Check feedwater pump, blowdown valve seating

EMERGENCY: Boiler trip on high pressure - DO NOT reset until cause identified
